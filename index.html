Why raccoons are my favorite! (:

I think raccoons are really cute. I really like their color palette, their eye masks, and stripes. I follow a lot of raccoon shelters on social media and enjoy watching the raccoons hashtag on instagram. I like how raccons can live in different places like forests and big cities.
